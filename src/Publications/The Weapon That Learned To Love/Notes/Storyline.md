# **The Weapon That Learned to Love**

# Chronological Backstories (Pre-Story Events)

## Robert’s Origin (The Weapon’s Creation)

- **Birth:** Born with a latent Natural Soma-Manifestation (extreme physical density/potential).
- **Young Adulthood:** Lives an isolated life. Graduates college and becomes a Software Developer.
- **The Turning Point:** Commits an accidental killing in self-defense. Realizes he feels no remorse and possesses unnatural talent for violence.
- **The Underworld:** Leaves his job. Becomes a freelancer (hitman). Gains reputation.
- **The Recruitment:** The World Council (WC) takes notice.
- **The Enhancement:** Recruited into the **Censors**. Undergoes Bio-Enhancement Program. His latent gene is activated and enhanced. He becomes a physical juggernaut.
- **The Breaking Point:** Witnesses the WC purge a civilian town to hide a failed experiment.
- **The Defection:** Robert deserts the WC.
- **Joining Nightfall (Age 56):** **Zero** recruits him into Nightfall.
- **The Mentor Era:** Serves as top operator and mentor.
- **The "Incident" (Age 62):** Robert loses control during a mission, brutally massacres enemies in front of students. He retires in shame.

---

## David’s Origin (The Anchor)

- **The Normal Life:** English teacher at Northwood High.
- **The Loss:** Eleanor's death.
- **The Void:** Falls into quiet depression. Lives monotonous life.
